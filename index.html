What is a "palindrome?"

A palindrome can be a word, a series of numbers or a sentence.
Its trait is that its contents are symmetrical.
Some examples are:

  • 123321
  • Otto
  • Do geese see God?
  • =＾● ⋏ ●＾=
  • 0_0 (: /-\ :) 0_0

Spaces, dots, commas and any other symbols are being ignored as you have noticed, except for when you only work with said symbols.
The palindrome can always be read forwards and backwards!
